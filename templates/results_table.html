About TimeBanking

TimeBanking Values

1. Assets

We all have something to give.

We all have something of value to contribute.

2. Redefining Work

Work should strengthen familes, build communities, promote equality and sustainability.

3. Reciprocity

The question: “How can I help you?” needs to change so we ask:

“How can we help each other build the world we all will live in?”.

4. Social Networks

People joined together in shared purpose are stronger than individuals and can create communities of support, strength & trust.

5. Respect

Work with people where they are now, not where we hope they will be at some future point.
